According to current research we humans often form beliefs after we take actions.

We can leverage knowledge of this brain feature for good.

Practicing small positive actions can lead to positive long term results in our thinking and habits.

But we must be intentional. We need to engage our thinking brain, not just our reacting brain.

Funny, scary, or angry memes propagate easily because of our emotional reactions. GoodMemes.Org memes are simple positive actions that require us to decide to engage our brains, perform a beneficial task, and purposefully re-share the meme. We're taking tiny steps to make things a little bit better. Join us!
